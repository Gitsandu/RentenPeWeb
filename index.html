Frequently asked questions

Tenants will receive a confirmation email and notification on the platform once their payment has been processed successfully.

Payment processing times may vary depending on the payment method chosen, but it typically takes 1-3 business days for payments to be processed.

Free Rent amount will be deducted for missing a rent payment on the platform. It is important to ensure timely payment to avoid these fees.

Tenants will receive a notification if their payment is declined and may need to update their payment information or choose a different payment method.

Tenants can upload their rental agreement through their My agreements, where they can attach the document and link their landlord to it.
