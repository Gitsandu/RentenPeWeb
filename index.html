Pay Rent

At the heart of the app is the Pay Rent feature. Located at the back of the R Card it is activated post Tenant KYC and rent agreement validation. Users can pay rent using multiple payment gateways. Now redeem your accumulated rewards points from various bank credit and debit cards to offset your rent payments.

Residence card

The Residence card (R-Card), a sophisticated ID card, serves as the pinnacle of convenience and security in the rental ecosystem. It is a hallmark of trust between a tenant and a landlord. The R card establishes integrity of tenant’s rent payment eliminating the need for interviews with landlords. R-Card helps Landlords, select the right tenant for their precious rental homes with ease on the RentenPe platform.

Gullak

Ensure your rental hygiene and engage in playful tasks to earn rewards which can be stored in your personal Gullak. The coins earned are the digital currency of RentenPe that can be redeemed towards your rent payment. Immerse yourself in fun activities such as quizzes, selfies, and games to unlock exciting rewards.

R Wish

A unique feature, empowers both Tenants & Landlords by providing convenient access to short-term personal loans with just a few clicks. Such loans could be used by tenants and landlords to meet rental related emergencies.

R Saftey

Acting as a shield, R Safety offers several measures to safeguard landlords’ precious rental property. A deep background verification check, instant access to legal and property experts via intelligent chat, access to powerful rental analytics throwing light on the region’s rental ecosystem and many more rich services could be availed on a subscription basis.

Chat

RentenPe’s intelligent chat is a free-flow communication channel for both tenant and landlords to discuss rent related matters. When needed, it enables a tension-free arbitration between the landlord and tenant moderated by an external legal professional.

Locker

The Locker is a secure mechanism to preserve and share vital documents between landlords and tenants on the RentenPe platform. The Locker provides easy and secure means to share documents or their links with the outside world through popular channels such as WhatsApp, Email, and SMS. The history of share can be traced back to in time to know the underlying context of sharing.
